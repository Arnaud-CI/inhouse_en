Greater efficiency

The internal volume of the HyCare™ pump is 50% smaller thanks to the innovative design of the body and the Duraflex flexible shaft.

Replacing a H pump, 90H12, by HyCare™ can generate a cost saving of 35550 € per year.

Taking into account that by the end of production and despite a water flush, the volume of the product in the pump body is diluted and consequently not used. 50 weeks of production per year, 2 water flush or CIP per day, 3 € / kg pumped product, density 1,1..
